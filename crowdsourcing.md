# Crowdsourcing

Another broad and recurring area of interest is crowdsourcing and collaborative data. The projects which I have contributed to most significantly are Wikipedia, OpenStreetMaps, Niantic Wayfarer, and MusixMatch. Beyond these, I have contributed to various Fandom wikis and will frequently report bugs when I see them in other pieces of software.

I have, for some reason, and indefatigable urge to make things a little better than I found them. One likely source of this is Boy Scouts' *Leave No Trace* principles. While designed primarily for the natural world, they are portable to much of our digital environs. Too often, I think we accept that the internet and her multitudinous information silos do not work properly. It is worth man's time and effort to revisit, revise, and improve upon what we have.

Let's make things a little more useful for whomever will use them next.
